Founded by WONDERWORLD PRESCHOOL AND DAYCAREE, Teaching Trailblazers offers science-backed, hands-on, standards-based curriculum, professional development, and teaching resources. Our mission is to elevate early childhood education as a profession, and to provide the leadership, support, and resources pre-k and preschool teachers need to help their students succeed. For more than half a century, we have offered a caring and supportive environment that inspires and challenges young learners to fulfill their potential in a variety of ways. Here, the excitement for learning is palpable among teachers and students alike. You'll find our commitment to developing character and critical thinking to be a common theme, from the carefully curated curriculum to the passionate, devoted teachers who bring it all to life. Together, they offer a perfect balance of academic rigor and creative exploration that makes the most of each childs unique gifts and interests. Put simply, we give your child the tools to communicate, think, act and succeed in this complex, ever-changing world.
